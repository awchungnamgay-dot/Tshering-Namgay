PARO

Paro

Paro is one of the most popular and picturesque regions of Bhutan, located in the western part of the country. It is well known for its rich history, beautiful valleys, traditional architecture, and important cultural landmarks. Paro is surrounded by green mountains, peaceful rivers, and fertile farmland, making it one of the most scenic places in Bhutan. The district is home to the famous Paro Taktsang, also known as the Tiger’s Nest Monastery, which is one of Bhutan’s most sacred and visited sites. Paro also contains Rinpung Dzong and Bhutan’s only international airport, Paro International Airport. The region plays an important role in Bhutanese culture, tourism, and religion while preserving its traditional lifestyle and natural beauty.

Hotels Available

COMO Uma, Paro Dewachen Resort, Paro Paro Heritage Hotel, Paro

THIMPHU

Thimphu is the capital and largest city of Bhutan, located in the western part of the country. It is the political, economic, and cultural center of Bhutan, where modern development blends beautifully with traditional Bhutanese culture. Thimphu is known for its clean environment, peaceful atmosphere, colorful architecture, and scenic mountain views. The city is home to important landmarks such as Tashichho Dzong, Buddha Dordenma, and Memorial Chorten, which attract many visitors every year. Thimphu also serves as the main center for education, government offices, businesses, and tourism in Bhutan while still preserving the country’s unique traditions and values.

Hotels Available

Le Méridien, Thimphu Phuntsho Pelri, Thimphu Six Senses, Thimphu

BUMTHANG

Bumthang

Bumthang is one of the most beautiful and culturally rich regions of Bhutan, located in the central part of the country. Known as the spiritual heartland of Bhutan, Bumthang is famous for its ancient monasteries, peaceful valleys, traditional villages, and stunning natural scenery. The district is surrounded by green mountains, pine forests, and flowing rivers, creating a calm and refreshing environment. It is also home to many important religious sites such as Jakar Dzong and Kurjey Lhakhang, which attract both tourists and pilgrims. The people of Bumthang are known for preserving Bhutanese traditions, culture, and local crafts, making the region an important center of Bhutanese heritage.

Hotels Available

Amankora, Bumthang Hotel Jakar, Bumthang Hotel Ugyenling, Bumthang
